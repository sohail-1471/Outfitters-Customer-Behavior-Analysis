# 📊 Outfitters Customer Shopping Behavior Dashboard – Power BI Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes customer shopping behavior for a Pakistani fashion brand, Outfitters. It provides insights into customer preferences, purchasing patterns, and engagement levels to support better business and marketing decisions.

## 🎯 Objectives

* To understand customer shopping behavior
* To analyze purchasing patterns and preferences
* To evaluate customer engagement through ratings and subscriptions
* To identify trends across gender and product categories
* To build an interactive and visually appealing dashboard

## 📈 Key Metrics (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights the following key indicators:

* **Total Customers**
* **Average Purchase Amount**
* **Average Rating**

These KPIs provide a quick overview of customer activity and satisfaction.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes multiple visualizations to explore customer data:

* **Subscription Status**
  Shows the distribution of subscribed vs non-subscribed customers.

* **Customer Distribution by Gender**
  Provides insights into gender-based shopping patterns.

* **Product Category Analysis**
  Displays which product categories are most popular among customers.

* **Shipping Type Analysis**
  Highlights preferred shipping methods used by customers.

* **Customer Ratings**
  Reflects customer satisfaction and feedback trends.

## 🧰 Tools & Techniques Used

* Power BI Desktop
* Data cleaning and transformation
* Data modeling
* Interactive filters and slicers
* KPI cards for quick insights
* Various charts (bar charts, pie charts, line charts, etc.)

## 🎨 Dashboard Elements

* **KPI Cards** for key performance indicators
* **Charts & Graphs** for behavior and trend analysis
* **Filters/Slicers** for dynamic user interaction
* **Clean and intuitive layout** for better usability

## 🔍 Insights Gained

* Identification of high-value customer segments
* Understanding of purchasing behavior by gender
* Popular product categories among customers
* Customer preferences for shipping methods
* Relationship between ratings and customer satisfaction

This project demonstrates how Power BI can be used to analyze customer shopping behavior and extract meaningful insights. It highlights the importance of data-driven strategies in improving customer experience and business performance.
